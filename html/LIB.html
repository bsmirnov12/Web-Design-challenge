Liberal Party

The plot shows dramatic history of Liberal Party in past decade. Decline of 2011, resulting in a drop to the third place in parliament representation, was probably caused by political leadership of Michael Ignatieff. As dramatic was a sudden triumphal raise to the top in 2015, thanks to succession of a new charismatic leader Justin Trudeau.

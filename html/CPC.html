Conservative Party

Steady raise of the power from 2006 to 2015 is often attributed to party leader Stephen Harper, who took Canada through turbulent years of the world economic crisis. However, the party couldn't stand against Liberals in 2015, lost majority and ended up second. Leadership reelection in 2017, seems didn't help much, because Liberals are still ahead by good margin. Anyway, I should recognize their stability - since its foundation in 2003 the party never droped below second place during elections.
